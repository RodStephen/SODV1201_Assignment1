About Me

Born in the heart of the Philippines, Rod Stephen or you can call him Rod for short has spent his formative years surrounded by the rich culture, warm hospitality, and natural beauty. Currently embarking on an exciting academic journey as an international student in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

I'm passionately pursuing a Software Development Diploma at Bow Valley College, where I delve into the intricacies of coding, design, and innovation. Amidst the snowy landscapes of Calgary, I find myself navigating through lines of code and embracing the challenges and triumphs of honing my skills in this dynamic field. From the bustling streets of Santiago to the lively atmosphere of Calgary, I'm on a mission to blend my Filipino roots with the diverse opportunities presented by my Canadian education.
